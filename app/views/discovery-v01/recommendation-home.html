Understand how to make a complaint about a school

This tool supports those who have a complaint about a school, and want to know what to do next.

Most issues can be resolved quickly when you know who to speak to, and what to expect.

Different organisations in the education system have different areas of responsibility, and may be able to help with your complaint.

Speak to the school first

Parents and schools share the same goal: supporting children's education, but sometimes questions or concerns arise.

Often issues are best resolved by a child's teacher, or school leadership. If needed, each school should have a complaint procedure you can follow.

In most cases, you should try to resolve your complaint with the school before any other organisation can get involved.
