Contact the Department for Education

DfE will consider your complaint.

You can submit a complaint to DfE through their help portal.

Use this service to complain about a local authority maintained school or an academy school if:

  • you're not happy with the way the school handled your complaint
  • the school is stopping you from completing their complaints procedure
  • a child is not getting an education
  • a child has been exposed to harm

DfE may use the information you provide to recommend improvements to school policies.

DfE must refer your complaint to other appropriate organisations if it raises concerns about the safety or wellbeing of a child.

What DfE cannot help with

The DfE cannot normally change the school's decision on a specific issue. Their focus is on whether the school has an effective complaints management process.
