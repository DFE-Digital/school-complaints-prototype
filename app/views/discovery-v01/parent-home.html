Complain about a school in England

Use this form to submit a formal complaint about your child’s school.

Each school should have a complaint procedure you can follow which is available on their website, or on request. This will include details specific to each school.

Speak to the school first

Often issues are best resolved by a child's teacher, or school leadership.

Parents and schools share the same goal: supporting children's education, but sometimes questions or concerns arise.

In most cases, you should try to discuss your concern before raising a formal complaint.

See Parentkind's checklist to help parents quickly, easily, and positively resolve concerns with their child’s school.

Contact the police if anyone is in danger

Call the police if a child or adult is in danger, or you believe a crime has been committed.

Call 999 in an emergency, or call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 to report something anonymously.
